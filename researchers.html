Research Cycle

Timeline

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. The research cycle runs from October 2026 through Symposium Day in Spring 2027.

September 2026
Interest Registration

Submit your interest via the registration form. Share your research area and any initial ideas. Mentor matching begins.

October 2026
Research Kickoff

Orientation session and mentor introductions. Narrow your research question with mentor guidance. Access the EBS resource library.

October – January
Active Research Period

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet. Biweekly check-ins with your mentor. Literature review, data collection or analysis, and iterative development of your project.

February 2027
Abstract Submission

Submit a 250-word abstract describing your research question, methodology, findings, and conclusions. Mentor review encouraged before submission.

March – April 2027
Presentation Preparation

Prepare your poster or short oral talk. Receive feedback from mentors and the organizing team. Practice Q&A with peers.

Spring 2027
Symposium Day

Present your research at Phillips Exeter Academy. Receive judge feedback, attend guest speaker talks, and connect with the broader EBS community.

How You Present

Presentation Formats

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Choose the format that best fits your project.

Poster Presentation

Design a research poster and present it to judges and attendees during the poster session. Judges spend approximately 15 minutes per poster. Standard academic poster format — EBS provides a template.

Recommended for first-timers

Short Oral Talk

Deliver a 10-minute presentation followed by a 5-minute Q&A to a small audience of judges and peers. Slide-based format. Indicate your preference on the registration form — oral slots are limited.

Limited slots
