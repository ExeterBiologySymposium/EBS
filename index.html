Spotlight: Spring 2027

Starting on the green hills of Exeter, a new community of high-school researchers is taking its first step into biology. The Exeter Biology Symposium opens dry-lab research to students with no prior experience — and connects them with mentors, peers, and judges who care.

Why EBS Exists

Many Exeter students want to do research but don't know where to start
Traditional programs often require prior experience or lab access
Dry-lab research is just as rigorous and accessible without a laboratory
Scientific communication is a skill learned through practice and community
Connecting with professionals early opens doors for future research careers
